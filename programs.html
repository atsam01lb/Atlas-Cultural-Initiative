Five formats, one mission

Our programs

Each format is a different doorway into another culture. Mix and match. Some are built for venues with a stage, others fit a quiet corner café.

01

Cultural themed nights

The music, language, food, traditions and history of one country, in one evening. Curated playlists, signature dishes, mini language primers and cultural storytelling — all in a single venue, in a single night.

02

Language exchange

Workshops where conversation crosses borders. Native speakers and learners pair up across tables, swap languages, and leave with new connections. No teachers, no curriculum — just curiosity.

03

Art-themed workshops

Hands-on sessions exploring the visual traditions of cultures around the world — calligraphy, textile patterns, ceramics, illustration. Materials provided. No prior experience needed.

04

Cultural workshops

Traditional games, customs and culture-related prizes — playful, social, immersive. From mahjong nights to henna circles, these are designed to be tried, not just watched.

05

VR virtual tours

Step into a chosen country through immersive VR. Walk Marrakech's medina, stand under Iceland's northern lights, visit Kyoto in cherry blossom — all without leaving your seat.
