About Atlas

For everyone in Lebanon — locals, expats, travelers, and the international community.

Atlas is an independent cultural platform launching in Lebanon, with regional ambition. We don't own a venue — we activate the ones already alive: cafés, restaurants, pubs, cultural centers and activity spaces.

Our events are designed to be welcoming across nationalities, languages and ages. Some are free. Some are ticketed. All of them open a door.
